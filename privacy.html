COOKIES

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Evolve Servers uses cookies to help Evolve Servers identify and track visitors, their usage of Evolve Servers website, and their website access preferences.
